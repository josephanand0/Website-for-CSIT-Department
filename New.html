Dr. H N Lakshmi
Head of the Department

  • Professor and Head of CSIT, has more than 19 years of teaching experience. She has Completed Ph.D (CS) from University of Hyderabad in 2016 and M.S (Software Systems) from BITS, Pilani in 1999. Her areas of interest include Web Services, Analysis of Algorithms, Data Structures and Programming. She has authored more than 12 research papers, one book chapter in Springer, out of which 4 papers are DBLP and 6 are Scopus indexed, and have around 20 citations.
